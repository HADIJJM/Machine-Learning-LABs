# Electric Vehicle (EV) Market Dataset (2010–2024)

## Overview
This dataset provides a comprehensive snapshot of the global electric vehicle (EV) market from 2010 to 2024. It contains historical data on EV sales, stock levels, and market share across multiple countries, enabling in-depth analysis of adoption trends and market evolution.

## Dataset Contents
The dataset includes the following key dimensions:

- Country – Geographic region of the recorded data  
- Year – Time range from 2010 to 2024  
- Vehicle Mode – Type of vehicle (e.g., cars)  
- Powertrain Type –  
  - BEV: Battery Electric Vehicles  
  - PHEV: Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles  
- Metric Type –  
  - Sales  
  - Stock  
  - Market Share  

## Purpose and Use Cases
This dataset is suitable for:

- Time-series analysis of EV adoption trends  
- Comparative market share studies across countries  
- Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- Forecasting and predictive modeling  
- Policy and market research  

## Example Applications
- Analyzing global EV market growth over time  
- Comparing BEV vs PHEV adoption rates  
- Studying country-level EV penetration  
- Building interactive dashboards for EV trends  

## Notes
- Data spans from 2010 to 2024.
- Data is aggregated at the country and year levels.
- Some values may be estimated based on available reports and official statistics.
